Kimchi Jjigae

Description

this is a simple recipe for a traditional Korean comfort food. Beware yuppies. This is spicy and fermented. Not for the faint of heart!

Directions

  1. fry the kimchi
  2. pour water until kimchi is covered
  3. when water has come to a boil, add everything else
  4. let it sit until it's ready
  5. enjoy with rice!

this is adapted from my grandma's traditional recipe!
