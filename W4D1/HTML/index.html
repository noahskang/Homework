Description

this is a simple recipe for a traditional Korean comfort food. Beware yuppies. This is spicy and fermented. Not for the faint of heart! Wikipedia link

Ingredients

  • Kimchi
  • Tofu
  • Green Onions
  • Spam
  • Red Pepper
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil

Directions

  1. fry the kimchi
  2. pour water until kimchi is covered
  3. when water has come to a boil, add everything else
  4. let it sit until it's ready
  5. enjoy with rice!

this is adapted from my grandma's traditional recipe!
